About Us

Trance Kafka, aka "peanut butter music," fuses disco, punk, electronica, and trip hop into a sticky, danceable mix made for one thing: booty shaking! With their debut EP “Jokes Aside” just dropped and with shows in and beyond Stockholm—this is just the beginning.

Members of the band

  • Chloé Astruc - Vocals
  • Marcos Correa - Vocals, guitar
  • Åke Gauffin - Lead guitar
  • Antoine Sauvagnac - Bass
  • Ludvig Larsson - Drums
